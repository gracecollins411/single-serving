About

Grace Collins has been passionate about photography for almost her whole life. She finds it as a way to capture memories forever. Her favorite pictures to take are usually of her friends, family, and places she loves. As you can see from above, Grace has made a collection of all of her favorite memories. Photography helps her remember the good moments in life. Each photo captures a moment unlike any other. Grace has been to many programs in order to improve her photography skills. She went to Interlochen Arts Camp in Michigan in order to learn more about film photography, especially working with black and white. In high school she took a digital photography class in which she learn all about how cameras function. She also learned techniques on how to create good composition in a photo. In college she took a digital photography class as well in which she learned more about the history of photography and how to improve her skills. Throughtout the years Grace has tried her best to improve her photography skills while also making them meaningful to her.
